    Wet Cat Food

    Wet cat food continues to be the most nutritious option for cats and kittens. Cats are carnivores, which means they love and require a diet that is rich in meats. Wet cat food offers cuddly kitties the animal protein they crave and deserve. Cats love the taste of wet food, but did you know it has health benefits, too? It helps keep your kitty hydrated, which is important for maintaining proper health. Wild prey can contain as much as 75% water, which is why cats have evolved to obtain water through the food they eat. Water plays a major role in felines every bodily function, especially digestion. Cats tend to not drink enough water on their own to ensure proper hydration. A simple solution to avoid dehydration is to add canned cat food to your furry felines eating routine.
