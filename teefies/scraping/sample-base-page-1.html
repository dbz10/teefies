    Description

    Treat your kitty to a whisker-licking bowl of bistro-inspired flavors with Fancy Feast's Classic Poultry & Beef Feast Variety Pack. This multi-pack comes with three finely ground, smooth pate recipes, including Tender Liver & Chicken, Turkey & Giblet and Tender Beef & Chicken. Each recipe is crafted with real meat as the very first ingredient and provides your kitty with the 100% complete and balanced nutrition she needs. So, open up the easy-pull tab and watch your cat lick her dish clean. After all, variety is the spice of life!

    • Provides a 100% complete and balanced meal with real meat as the very first ingredient.

    • Enhanced with vitamins and minerals for overall health and well-being.
    • Each recipe is suitable for both kittens and cats, giving them the delicious nutrition they deserve.

    Feeding Instructions

    Feed an average size adult cat 1 can per 2 1/2 lbs. of body weight daily. Feed up to twice this amount to kittens. Pregnant or nursing cats may require two to four times their normal feeding.

    Transition Instructions

    When introducing a pet to a new food, we recommend a gradual change over a 10 day period. Start by feeding 90% of their old food and 10% of Fancy Feast. Each day, increase their portion of Fancy Feast by 10%, while decreasing their old food until 100% of their meal consists of their new food.
